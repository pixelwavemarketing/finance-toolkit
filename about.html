About Open Finance Calculators

We believe everyone deserves access to professional-grade financial tools and educational content, regardless of their financial situation.

Our Mission

To democratize financial planning by providing free, accurate, and educational financial calculators that help people make informed decisions about their money.

Our Values

  • Privacy Friendly: All calculations run in your browser. We never see your financial data.
  • Educational Focus: We don't just calculate - we teach you the math and concepts behind each calculation.
  • Professional Quality: Our tools meet the same standards used by financial professionals.
  • Accessibility: Free tools that work on any device, anywhere, anytime.

Why We're Different

Unlike many financial websites that require signups, subscriptions, or bombard you with ads, we focus purely on providing valuable tools and education. Our calculators are designed to be:

  • Comprehensive: Cover all major personal finance scenarios
  • Accurate: Use industry-standard formulas and calculations
  • Educational: Include detailed explanations and math breakdowns
  • Mobile-First: Optimized for all devices and screen sizes
  • Offline-Capable: Work even without internet after first visit

Our Story

Open Finance Calculators was created by a team passionate about financial literacy and technology. We recognized that while there are many financial calculators online, most either lack educational content, require personal information, or are buried in ads.

We set out to create something different - a comprehensive suite of financial tools that not only calculate but also educate, all while maintaining user privacy and professional standards.
