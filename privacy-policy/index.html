Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Open Finance Calculators collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Good news: We collect virtually no personal information.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Financial Data: We never see, store, or transmit your financial information
  • Personal Details: No names, addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Usage Patterns: We don't track how you use our calculators
  • Cookies: No tracking cookies or user profiling

What We May Collect (Minimal):

  • Server Logs: Basic server access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Analytics: Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve our service
  • Contact Form: Only if you choose to contact us (name, email, message)

How We Use Information

Server Logs:

  • Monitor website performance and security
  • Identify and prevent abuse or technical issues
  • Improve website reliability and speed

Analytics:

  • Understand which calculators are most popular
  • Identify areas for improvement
  • Make our tools more user-friendly

Contact Information:

  • Respond to your questions and feedback
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our calculators based on your input

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement several measures to protect any information we do collect:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • Secure Hosting: Our servers are hosted on secure, enterprise-grade infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Limited access to any collected data, only for essential operations
  • Regular Updates: We keep our systems updated with the latest security patches

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services to provide our calculators:

Google Services:

  • Google Ads: For displaying relevant advertisements (with your consent)
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics (with your consent)
  • Google Search Console: For website performance monitoring

Formspree:

  • Handles contact form submissions
  • Your contact information is only shared if you choose to use the contact form
  • Formspree has their own privacy policy for data they process

Note: All third-party services are configured to respect your privacy preferences and comply with applicable data protection regulations.

Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any personal data we may have
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for any processing that requires it

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our calculators are designed for educational purposes and may be used by individuals of all ages, including students learning about personal finance.

However:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us
  • We will promptly delete any such information upon verification

Changes to This Policy

  • The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly

Contact Us

We're committed to transparency and will be happy to address any concerns you may have about your privacy.
