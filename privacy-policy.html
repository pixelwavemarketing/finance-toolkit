Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

What We Do

Open Finance Calculators provides free financial calculators that run entirely in your browser. We don't store your calculation data or require accounts.

Data We Don't Collect

  • Your calculations: All math happens in your browser—we never see your numbers
  • Personal information: No names, emails, or account creation required
  • Financial data: Your investment amounts, debts, and scenarios stay private

Data That May Be Collected

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to show relevant ads. Google may collect:

  • Browser type and settings
  • IP address (for location-based ads)
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Ad interaction data

Google uses cookies and similar technologies. You can control ad personalization in your Google Ads Settings.

Analytics (if applicable)

We may use Google Analytics to understand site usage:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device and browser information
  • General location (city/region level)
  • Traffic sources (where visitors came from)

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential functions: Remembering your last calculator settings
  • Advertising: Google AdSense cookies for relevant ads
  • Analytics: Understanding how the site is used

Your Choices

  • Browser settings: Block or delete cookies in your browser
  • Ad blocking: Use ad blockers to prevent ad-related tracking
  • Google settings: Opt out of personalized ads at adssettings.google.com
  • Do Not Track: We respect browser Do Not Track signals

Data Security

Since we don't store your data, there's nothing to breach. Your calculations happen locally and aren't transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our site doesn't target children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
