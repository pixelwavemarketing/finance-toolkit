Frequently Asked Questions

Are your calculators free to use?

Yes! All of our calculators are completely free to use. No registration, no accounts, no hidden fees.

Do you store my financial information?

No, we never store your financial data. All calculations happen locally in your browser, and we don't collect or transmit any of your information.

How accurate are your calculations?

Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and are designed to provide accurate results for financial planning purposes. However, they should be used as educational tools, not as the sole basis for financial decisions.

Can I use your calculators on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our calculators are fully responsive and work great on all devices, including smartphones and tablets.

Do you offer financial advice?

We provide educational tools and information, but we don't offer personalized financial advice. For specific financial decisions, we recommend consulting with a qualified financial advisor.

Response Time

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.
